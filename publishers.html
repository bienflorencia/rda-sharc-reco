Encourage OA publishing in all LMICs1 by revising the criteria for publishing fees and adjust it using meaningful indicators

For instance: To the national Gross domestic expenditure on R&D (GERD) and not only to the country GDP (e.g. Uruguay is considered high income country although science funding is 0.48% of total GDP in 2019)
